The Janky

& The Glamour

14 years on the pole and I'm just getting started. What began as survival became revolution. I'm Reagan—educator, agitator, and your favorite stripper with an MFA from CalArts.

My work lives in the tension between the "janky"—the raw, DIY, imperfect reality of sex work—and the "glamour": the rhinestoned, spotlighted, unapologetic performance of femininity that pays my rent and fuels my art.

I organize with grassroots collectives fighting for decriminalization, lecture on labor rights in the adult industry, and create performance art that makes museum-goers uncomfortable in the best way possible.
